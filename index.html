Our story

Beard and You is a French brand offering cosmetic products for men's beard. The brand is committed, it produces its products in France with natural products and in agreement with nature. It is by combining passion and innovation that we want to give confidence to the men who use our products. The mission of Beard and You is to offer high quality cosmetic products, while respecting nature, human being and cultural diversity. "Reinvent yourself, take care of your beard": we want to create products for men that look like them and that are adapted to their skin and beard. We also want to make the pleasure of taking care of yourself accessible for everyone.
